Potato Chip Latkes

  • Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
large eggs 1
cup of water 1/4
tablespoon freshly sliced chives 1

Step 1: Place potato chips in a bowl, and crumble with your hands until finely crushed; you will need 1 cup crushed chips.

Step 2: To make the potato latke batter; whisk egg, milk, water, salt, flour, baking powder, black pepper, onions, and cayenne together in a large bowl until combined. Add the 1 cup of crushed potato chips and stir together until evenly combined.

Step 3: Wrap bowl and refrigerate until mixture has thickened, 30 to 60 minutes, or up to overnight.

Christmas Bark

  • Time: 30 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
cooking spray 1
ounces chocolate or vanilla flavor candy coating (almond bark), chopped 8
tablespoon shortening 1

Step 1:Gather all ingredients.

Step 2: Line a large baking sheet with heavy foil; grease foil with cooking spray. Combine candy coating, chocolate, and shortening in a large microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on High until chocolate is melted, stirring every 30 seconds, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes.

Step 3: Pour mixture onto the prepared baking sheet; spread out evenly, 1/4 inch thick.
